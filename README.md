## Description

First person driving game that simulates the lived experiences of the Bipolar Personality Disorder. The player drives around the city, reaching landmarks within the given time frame. They must navigate the map while experiencing unexpected state changes; their regular state euthymia, depressive episodes, and manic episodes.

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

Important keys:
W: Drive forward
S: Reverse
A: Steer left
D: Steer right
Shift: Boost
Space bar: Pause and start the game
M: Reduced effects during manic episodes

Instructions:
Complete a new trip around the city each level by following the mission bar instructions, all while racing against the timer. Use the minimap and HUD hints for navigation support.

The game will pass through three states as the player drives; their occurrence cannot be controlled or predicted.
Euthymia: The map maintains its regular appearance and the mini map works as normal. The car moves as expected.
Manic: The mini map rotates and the car will feel much faster and difficult to control.
Depression: The mini map is obscured, except for the areas the player has already traversed. The car will feel heavy and slow, and the map will appear elongated and distorted.

Accessibility Options:
Reduced Effects Mode: The mini map will not rotate during Manic episodes

## Iteration Notes (Post-Playtest)

Removed obstructions on the map: Reduced hitbox sizes and removed lampposts for easier player navigation
Reduced effects mode: Added the M key Toggle that stops minimap rotation during manic episodes for people affected by motion sickness
Map walkthrough: Added a map walkthrough in the Tutorial level to help players familiarize themselves with the game environment and improve their morale
